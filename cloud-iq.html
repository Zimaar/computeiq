Market Structure (March 2026): The AI cloud ecosystem is bifurcated into three distinct layers — (1) AI Factories & Reserved Frontier Clusters (CoreWeave, Nebius, Lambda, Crusoe, Oracle) offering H100/B200 at scale; (2) Self-Serve Specialist GPU Clouds (RunPod, Hyperstack, Vast.ai, TensorDock, io.net) providing price-floor access; and (3) Model / Inference API Platforms (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Cohere, Mistral, Groq, Fireworks). Beyond NVIDIA dominance, purpose-built accelerators — Google TPU v5, AWS Trainium2, Intel Gaudi 3, Groq LPU, Cerebras WSE — are capturing specialized workloads. Blackwell-era hardware (B200/GB200/GB300) is now available but mostly reserved/enterprise. H100 & H200 remain the production workhorses.
